Supplemental Instruction (SI) at TCC

Supplemental Instruction targets historically challenging courses. Rather than remediation, it provides peer-facilitated, collaborative study environments where students learn what to study alongside how to study.

Collaborative Workshops: Small-group interactive problems.

Peer Leaders: Guided by students who previously mastered the class.

Zero Cost: Completely free for all registered TCC students.

Strategic Leadership

Aligning academic programs with institutional curriculum goals to support student progression paths across TCC.

Program Development

Leader Mentorship

Training and cultivating peer facilitators in pedagogical theory, group management, and interactive leadership.

Impact Assessment
